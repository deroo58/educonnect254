History - Form I

Topics Covered

Natural Numbers

Identify, round off, classify, and solve problems involving natural numbers.

2. Factors

Express composite numbers in factor form and power form

3. Divisibility Tests

Test the divisibility of numbers by integers 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, 10, and 11.

4. Greatest Common Divisor (GCD) / Highest Common Factor (HCF)

Find GCD/HCF of numbers and apply to real life.

4. Least Common Multiple (LCM)

Find LCM of numbers and apply knowledge to real life situations.

Integers

Define, identify on number line, perform operations, apply to real life.

Fractions

Identify, convert, compare, operate on fractions, apply to real life.

Decimals

Convert fractions/decimals, recurring decimals, standard form, perform operations.

Squares and Square Roots

Find squares by multiplication/tables; find square roots by factor method/tables.

Algebraic Expressions

Use letters, simplify, factorise, remove brackets, evaluate, apply to life.

Rates, Ratio, Percentages and Proportion

Define, solve problems, apply to real life situations (direct/inverse proportion).

Length

State units, convert units, significant figures, find perimeter and circumference (arcs).

Area

State and convert units, calculate area of plane figures (regular/irregular), surface area.

Volume and Capacity

State units, convert units, calculate volume of solids, relate volume to capacity.

Mass, Density and Weight

Define mass and weight, state units, distinguish mass/weight, relate volume/mass/density.

Time

Convert units, relate 12/24 hour systems, read/solve travel time-tables.

Linear Equations

Solve linear and simultaneous equations (one/two unknowns), form equations from life.

Commercial Arithmetic 1

State/convert currency using exchange rates, calculate profit/loss, discount/commission.

Co-ordinates and Graphs

Draw cartesian plane, plot points, draw/interpret linear graphs, solve simultaneous equations.

Angles and Plane Figures

Identify types, solve problems involving angles (straight line/point/transversal/polygons).

Geometrical Constructions

Construct perpendicular bisectors, angles, polygons using ruler and compasses.

Scale Drawing

Interpret/choose scales, draw sketches/bearings, angles of elevation/depression, simple surveying.

Common Solids

Identify/sketch solids, draw/make nets, calculate surface area, find distance between points.
